## 3. INTEREST RATE

### 3.1 Contract Rate

The unpaid principal balance of this Note shall bear interest at the fixed annual rate of **[CONTRACT INTEREST RATE]%** per annum (the "Contract Rate"), calculated on the basis of a 360-day year and the actual number of days elapsed.

**Applicable ranges by loan type (for reference — select rate within applicable range):**
- Micro Loans ($10,000–$75,000): 14.00%–18.00% per annum
- Renovation/Rehab Loans ($75,000–$500,000): 12.00%–15.00% per annum
- Bridge Loans ($100,000–$2,000,000): 10.00%–14.00% per annum

### 3.2 Default Rate

Upon the occurrence and during the continuance of any Event of Default (as defined in Section 8), the entire outstanding principal balance of this Note shall bear interest at the rate of **twenty-four percent (24.00%) per annum** or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law in the state where the Property is located, whichever is lower (the "Default Rate"). The Default Rate shall apply automatically without notice or demand from Lender and shall continue until such Event of Default is cured to Lender's satisfaction or the Loan is repaid in full.

### 3.3 Computation of Interest

Interest shall be computed on the basis of a 360-day year consisting of twelve (12) months of thirty (30) days each, and shall be charged on the actual number of days elapsed for any partial month.

## 5. PAYMENT TERMS

### 5.1 Monthly Interest Payments

Commencing on the first day of the calendar month following the date of this Note, and continuing on the first day of each calendar month thereafter until the Maturity Date, Borrower shall make monthly payments of accrued interest only (each, a "Monthly Payment"). Each Monthly Payment shall be in the amount of the interest accrued during the preceding month on the outstanding principal balance.

### 5.2 Balloon Payment at Maturity

On the Maturity Date, the entire outstanding principal balance of this Note, together with all accrued and unpaid interest, all fees, costs, and other charges due hereunder or under the Loan Agreement and the Security Instrument, shall be due and payable in full (the "Balloon Payment").

### 5.3 Application of Payments

All payments received by Lender shall be applied in the following order: (a) first, to any late charges, fees, costs, and expenses then due; (b) second, to accrued and unpaid interest; and (c) third, to the outstanding principal balance.

### 5.4 Business Day Convention

If any payment date falls on a day that is not a Business Day (defined as a day on which commercial banks are open for business in the state where the Property is located), such payment shall be due on the next succeeding Business Day, and interest shall continue to accrue through such extended date.

## 6. EXTENSION PROVISIONS

### 6.1 Right to Request Extension

Borrower may request an extension of the Maturity Date by submitting a written request to Lender not less than ten (10) calendar days prior to the then-current Maturity Date. Any such extension shall be in the sole and absolute discretion of Lender.

### 6.2 Extension Terms

If Lender, in its sole discretion, agrees to extend the Maturity Date, the following terms shall apply:

**(a) Extension Period:** Each extension shall be for a period of thirty (30) days from the then-current Maturity Date (each, an "Extension Period").

**(b) Extension Fee:** As a condition to each extension, Borrower shall pay to Lender a non-refundable extension fee as follows:

- **Micro Loans:** 1.5–2.0 points of the outstanding principal balance per 30-day Extension Period
- **Renovation/Rehab Loans:** 1.5 points of the outstanding principal balance per 30-day Extension Period
- **Bridge Loans:** 1.5 points of the outstanding principal balance per 30-day Extension Period

The applicable extension fee for this Loan shall be **[EXTENSION FEE POINTS]** points per Extension Period, equal to **[EXTENSION FEE PERCENTAGE]%** of the then-outstanding principal balance (the "Extension Fee").

**(c) Payment of Extension Fee:** The Extension Fee shall be due and payable in full upon execution of a written extension agreement between Borrower and Lender. No extension shall be effective until the Extension Fee has been received by Lender.

**(d) Maximum Extensions:** The total number of Extension Periods shall not exceed **[MAXIMUM NUMBER OF EXTENSIONS]** without further written agreement of Lender.

### 6.3 Conditions to Extension

Each extension is conditioned upon: (a) no Event of Default having occurred and being continuing; (b) all interest payments being current; (c) Borrower's execution of an Extension Agreement in form acceptable to Lender; and (d) payment of the Extension Fee.

### 6.4 Holdover Penalty

If the Loan is not repaid on or before the Maturity Date (or the extended Maturity Date, if applicable) and no extension has been executed, the outstanding balance shall automatically begin accruing interest at the Default Rate set forth in Section 3.2, in addition to any other remedies available to Lender.

### 7.2 Micro Loans

If this Note evidences a Micro Loan, the following prepayment terms apply:

**(a) First 30 Days:** If Borrower prepays this Note in whole or in part within the first thirty (30) days following the date of this Note, Borrower shall pay a prepayment penalty equal to one (1) point (1.00%) of the principal amount prepaid (the "Prepayment Penalty").

**(b) After 30 Days:** After the first thirty (30) days following the date of this Note, Borrower may prepay this Note in whole or in part at any time without penalty or premium.

### 7.3 Minimum Interest

## 8. LATE CHARGE

If any Monthly Payment or other amount due under this Note is not received by Lender within **five (5) calendar days** after the date on which such payment is due (the "Grace Period"), Borrower shall pay to Lender a late charge equal to the greater of: (a) **five percent (5.00%)** of the overdue amount; or (b) **Fifty Dollars ($50.00)** (the "Late Charge"). The Late Charge shall be immediately due and payable, and shall be in addition to, and not in lieu of, any interest or other amounts due hereunder. The assessment of a Late Charge shall not constitute a waiver of any Event of Default arising from Borrower's failure to make timely payment.

## 9. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default" under this Note:

**(a)** Borrower fails to make any payment of principal, interest, or any other amount due under this Note within five (5) calendar days after the date on which such payment is due;

**(b)** Borrower fails to perform or observe any covenant, condition, or agreement contained in this Note, the Loan Agreement, the Security Instrument, or any other document or instrument executed in connection with the Loan (collectively, the "Loan Documents"), and such failure continues for ten (10) calendar days after written notice from Lender (except for payment obligations, which have a five (5) day grace period);

**(c)** Any representation or warranty made by Borrower in any Loan Document proves to have been false or misleading in any material respect when made;

**(d)** Borrower (i) files a voluntary petition in bankruptcy or is adjudicated bankrupt or insolvent; (ii) files any petition or answer seeking reorganization, arrangement, composition, readjustment, liquidation, dissolution, or similar relief under any present or future federal or state statute, law, or regulation; (iii) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors; (iv) becomes the subject of an involuntary petition in bankruptcy that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days of filing; or (v) has a receiver, trustee, custodian, or liquidator appointed for all or any substantial part of Borrower's properties;

**(e)** A judgment or judgments in excess of **$[JUDGMENT THRESHOLD]** in the aggregate are entered against Borrower and remain unsatisfied, unvacated, unbonded, or unstayed for a period of thirty (30) days;

**(f)** The Property is materially damaged or destroyed and Borrower fails to restore the Property or insurance proceeds are insufficient and not supplemented by Borrower;

**(g)** Any lien or encumbrance not approved by Lender is placed on the Property;

**(h)** Borrower transfers, conveys, or disposes of the Property or any interest therein without Lender's prior written consent (a "Transfer"), including any change in the controlling ownership interest of Borrower if Borrower is an entity;

**(i)** Any other event of default occurs under any of the Loan Documents.

If Lender ever receives as interest an amount that would exceed the Maximum Lawful Rate, such amount that would be excessive interest shall be applied to the reduction of the unpaid principal balance of this Note and not to the payment of interest, or if such excessive interest exceeds the unpaid principal balance of this Note, such excess shall be refunded to Borrower.

In determining whether the interest contracted for, charged, or received by Lender exceeds the Maximum Lawful Rate, Lender may, to the extent permitted by applicable law: (a) characterize any payment that is not principal as an expense, fee, or premium rather than as interest; (b) exclude voluntary prepayments and the effect thereof; and (c) amortize, prorate, allocate, and spread in equal or unequal parts the total amount of interest throughout the contemplated term of this Note.
