## STATE-SPECIFIC INSTRUMENT NOTE

This template is drafted in the Deed of Trust form, primarily styled for **Texas** and **Tennessee** transactions. The following states in NexusBridge Lending's operating area use different security instruments:

| State | Instrument | Foreclosure Type |
|---|---|---|
| **Texas** | Deed of Trust | Non-judicial |
| **Tennessee** | Deed of Trust | Non-judicial |
| **North Carolina** | Deed of Trust | Non-judicial |
| **Mississippi** | Deed of Trust | Non-judicial |
| **Arkansas** | Deed of Trust (or Mortgage) | Non-judicial (Deed of Trust) / Judicial (Mortgage) |
| **Virginia** | Deed of Trust | Non-judicial |
| **Florida** | Mortgage | Judicial only |
| **Georgia** | Security Deed | Non-judicial |
| **Alabama** | Mortgage | Non-judicial (with power of sale) |
| **South Carolina** | Mortgage | Judicial only |
| **Louisiana** | Mortgage (Act of Mortgage) | Executory process or ordinary process |

**IMPORTANT:** State-specific versions of this instrument **must** be prepared by licensed local counsel before use in any actual transaction. This template provides the general framework and must be adapted to comply with the recording requirements, foreclosure procedures, homestead protections, and other laws of the state where the Property is located.

## 2. SECURED OBLIGATIONS

This Deed of Trust secures the following obligations (collectively, the "Secured Obligations"):

**(a)** The payment of the indebtedness evidenced by the Note, including principal, interest, late charges, default interest, prepayment premiums (if any), and all other sums due thereunder;

**(b)** The payment and performance of all obligations of Borrower under this Deed of Trust, the Loan Agreement, and all other Loan Documents;

**(c)** The payment of all sums advanced by Lender pursuant to this Deed of Trust to protect the security of this Deed of Trust, including advances for taxes, insurance, repairs, and protection of the Property;

**(d)** The payment of all costs and expenses, including reasonable attorneys' fees, incurred by Lender or Trustee in enforcing or protecting any rights under the Loan Documents; and

**(e)** The payment and performance of all future advances and future obligations, if any, of Borrower to Lender under any amendment, modification, renewal, or extension of the Note or any other Loan Document.

## 3. BORROWER'S COVENANTS

Borrower covenants and agrees as follows:

### 3.1 Payment of Secured Obligations

Borrower shall pay the Secured Obligations promptly when due, including all principal, interest, fees, and other charges due under the Note and the other Loan Documents.

### 3.2 Insurance

**(a)** Borrower shall keep the Property continuously insured against loss or damage by fire, windstorm, and such other hazards as Lender may require, in an amount not less than the full replacement cost of the Improvements or the outstanding principal balance of the Loan, whichever is greater, under policies in form and with insurance companies satisfactory to Lender.

**(b)** If the Property is located in a Special Flood Hazard Area as designated by the Federal Emergency Management Agency, Borrower shall maintain flood insurance in an amount satisfactory to Lender.

**(c)** All insurance policies shall name Lender as mortgagee and loss payee, as its interests may appear, and shall provide that such policies shall not be cancelled, materially modified, or allowed to expire without at least thirty (30) days' prior written notice to Lender.

**(d)** Borrower shall deliver to Lender certificates of insurance or copies of all policies, together with evidence of premium payment, upon request.

**(e)** In the event of loss, Borrower shall give immediate notice to Lender and to the insurance carrier. Lender may make proof of loss if not made promptly by Borrower. Insurance proceeds shall be applied to restoration of the Property or to the Secured Obligations, at Lender's election.

### 3.3 Taxes and Assessments

**(a)** Borrower shall pay, before delinquency, all taxes, assessments, charges, and levies imposed by any governmental authority upon the Property or the Secured Obligations, including without limitation all real property taxes, personal property taxes, water and sewer charges, and other governmental charges.

**(b)** Borrower shall deliver to Lender, upon request, evidence of payment of all such taxes and assessments.

**(c)** Borrower shall not suffer or permit any lien for unpaid taxes to attach to the Property, except for liens for taxes not yet due and payable.

### 3.4 Maintenance and Preservation of Property

**(a)** Borrower shall maintain the Property in good condition and repair, shall not commit or suffer any waste, impairment, or deterioration of the Property, and shall comply with all laws, ordinances, regulations, covenants, conditions, and restrictions affecting the Property.

**(b)** Borrower shall not remove, demolish, or materially alter any Improvement without the prior written consent of Lender, except for alterations that do not materially affect the value of the Property.

**(c)** Borrower shall complete promptly and in a workmanlike manner any Improvement that is under construction on the Property.

**(d)** If the Loan is for renovation or rehabilitation purposes, Borrower shall complete all improvements in accordance with the plans and specifications approved by Lender, within the timeframes established in the Loan Agreement.

### 3.5 Protection of Lender's Security

**(a)** If Borrower fails to perform any of the covenants or agreements contained in this Deed of Trust, or if any action or proceeding is commenced that affects the Property or title thereto or the interest of Lender therein, Lender may, at Lender's option, make such appearances, disburse such sums, and take such action as Lender deems necessary, in its sole discretion, to protect Lender's interest, including payment of taxes, insurance premiums, and costs of repair.

**(b)** Any amounts disbursed by Lender pursuant to this Section shall become additional Secured Obligations, shall bear interest at the Default Rate from the date of disbursement, and shall be secured by this Deed of Trust.

### 3.6 Inspection

Borrower shall permit Lender and its agents, employees, and contractors to enter upon and inspect the Property at all reasonable times upon reasonable notice (or without notice in the event of an emergency or Event of Default). For renovation/rehab loans, Borrower shall permit inspection prior to each draw disbursement.

### 3.7 Condemnation

**(a)** Borrower shall promptly notify Lender of any threatened or commenced condemnation or eminent domain proceeding affecting the Property.

**(b)** Lender shall be entitled to all compensation, awards, and other payments arising from any condemnation or taking of the Property or any part thereof. Lender may apply such proceeds to the Secured Obligations or to restoration of the Property, at Lender's election.

**(c)** Borrower hereby assigns to Lender all right, title, and interest in and to any such awards or payments, and authorizes Lender to collect and receive the same and to give proper receipts and acquittances therefor.

### 3.8 No Transfer

Borrower shall not sell, transfer, convey, assign, mortgage, encumber, pledge, or otherwise dispose of the Property or any interest therein, or suffer or permit the same, without the prior written consent of Lender. Any such transfer without Lender's consent shall constitute an Event of Default and Lender may, at its option, declare all Secured Obligations immediately due and payable. If Borrower is an entity, any transfer, assignment, or change in the controlling ownership interest of Borrower shall be deemed a transfer of the Property for purposes of this Section.

### 3.9 No Junior Liens

Borrower shall not create, incur, assume, or suffer to exist any lien, encumbrance, or security interest on the Property (other than this Deed of Trust and any other liens expressly permitted in the Loan Documents) without the prior written consent of Lender.

### 3.10 Environmental Compliance

**(a)** Borrower represents and warrants that, to Borrower's knowledge, the Property is not contaminated by any Hazardous Materials (as defined by applicable federal, state, or local law) in violation of any Environmental Law.

**(b)** Borrower shall not cause or permit the release, storage, treatment, generation, transportation, or disposal of any Hazardous Materials on, under, in, or about the Property in violation of any Environmental Law.

**(c)** Borrower shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lender and Trustee from and against all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from any breach of this Section or any environmental contamination of the Property.

## 4. ASSIGNMENT OF RENTS

### 4.1 Assignment

Borrower hereby absolutely and unconditionally assigns and transfers to Lender all rents, royalties, income, issues, profits, revenue, deposits, and other benefits derived from or arising in connection with the Property (collectively, the "Rents"). This assignment is an absolute assignment and not merely an assignment for additional security.

### 4.2 License to Collect

So long as no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing, Borrower shall have a revocable license to collect, receive, and use the Rents. Upon the occurrence and during the continuance of any Event of Default, Lender may, at its option and without notice, revoke such license and collect and receive the Rents directly.

### 4.3 Application of Rents

Upon revocation of Borrower's license, Lender may apply the Rents to the Secured Obligations in such order as Lender determines in its sole discretion, after deducting all costs and expenses of collection, including reasonable attorneys' fees.

## 5. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default" under this Deed of Trust:

**(a)** Any Event of Default under the Note, the Loan Agreement, or any other Loan Document;

**(b)** Borrower fails to perform or observe any covenant, condition, or agreement contained in this Deed of Trust and such failure continues for ten (10) calendar days after written notice from Lender (or five (5) calendar days for payment obligations);

**(c)** Any representation or warranty made by Borrower in this Deed of Trust or any other Loan Document proves to be false or misleading in any material respect;

**(d)** A lien, levy, execution, or attachment is filed against the Property that is not bonded, released, or discharged within thirty (30) days;

**(e)** Borrower abandons the Property;

**(f)** Borrower fails to maintain insurance or pay taxes as required herein;

**(g)** Any transfer or encumbrance of the Property in violation of this Deed of Trust; or

**(h)** Any other event or condition that constitutes an Event of Default under any of the Loan Documents.

---

## 6. REMEDIES UPON DEFAULT

### 6.1 Acceleration

Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, Lender may, at its option, declare all Secured Obligations immediately due and payable, without notice or demand except as required by applicable law.

### 6.2 Power of Sale / Foreclosure

**(a)** Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, Lender may instruct Trustee to sell the Property at public auction in accordance with the laws of the state where the Property is located. Trustee shall give notice of the time and place of sale as required by applicable law.

**(b)** At any such sale, Lender may bid and purchase the Property. Trustee shall deliver to the purchaser a trustee's deed conveying the Property without any covenant or warranty, express or implied.

**(c)** The proceeds of any sale shall be applied in the following order: (i) to the costs and expenses of sale, including Trustee's fees and reasonable attorneys' fees; (ii) to the Secured Obligations in such order as Lender determines; and (iii) the surplus, if any, to Borrower or to such other person or entity as may be entitled thereto by law.

**(d) Non-Judicial Foreclosure (where applicable):** In states permitting non-judicial foreclosure under a deed of trust, Lender may elect to foreclose by exercise of the power of sale granted herein, in accordance with the applicable state foreclosure statute. In Texas, foreclosure shall comply with Texas Property Code Sections 51.002 et seq. In Tennessee, foreclosure shall comply with Tennessee Code Annotated Sections 35-5-101 et seq.

**(e) Judicial Foreclosure:** In states requiring judicial foreclosure (Florida, South Carolina, Louisiana), or at Lender's election in any state, Lender may foreclose this instrument by judicial proceedings in accordance with applicable law.

### 6.3 Appointment of Receiver

Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, Lender shall be entitled, as a matter of right, without notice and without regard for the adequacy of the security for the Secured Obligations or the solvency of Borrower, to the appointment of a receiver to take possession of and to operate the Property and to collect and apply the Rents. The receiver shall have all powers that may be necessary or appropriate for the operation of the Property, as the court deems just and equitable. Borrower hereby irrevocably consents to such appointment.

### 6.4 Entry and Possession

Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, Lender may enter upon and take possession of the Property, with or without legal process, and may manage and operate the Property, collect the Rents, and apply the same to the Secured Obligations.

### 6.5 Other Remedies

Lender shall have all other rights and remedies provided in this Deed of Trust, the Note, the Loan Agreement, the other Loan Documents, and by applicable law, including without limitation the rights and remedies of a secured party under the Uniform Commercial Code of the state where the Property is located. All rights and remedies shall be cumulative and may be exercised concurrently or in such order as Lender may determine.

### 6.6 Deficiency

If the proceeds of any sale are insufficient to pay all Secured Obligations in full, Borrower shall remain liable for any deficiency, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

No waiver of any provision of this Deed of Trust shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the waiving party. No waiver of any breach shall be deemed a waiver of any subsequent breach.

### 9.7 Entire Agreement

This Deed of Trust, together with the Note, the Loan Agreement, and the other Loan Documents, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the matters set forth herein. This Deed of Trust may not be amended except by a written instrument executed by Borrower and Lender.

### 9.8 Usury Savings

Notwithstanding any provision of this Deed of Trust to the contrary, in no event shall the interest collected or contracted for in connection with the Secured Obligations exceed the maximum rate permitted by applicable law. The usury savings provisions of the Note are incorporated herein by reference. If the Property is located in the State of Arkansas, no interest rate shall exceed the maximum permitted under the Arkansas Constitution, Amendment 89, Section 3.

### 9.9 Future Advances

This Deed of Trust secures, in addition to the obligations described herein, any and all future advances made by Lender to Borrower, to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

### 9.10 Fixture Filing

This Deed of Trust constitutes a fixture filing under the Uniform Commercial Code of the state where the Property is located with respect to any and all fixtures included within the Property.
