**(b) Covenant Default.** Borrower fails to perform or observe any covenant, condition, or agreement contained in this Agreement or any other Loan Document (other than a payment obligation), and such failure continues for ten (10) calendar days after written notice from Lender; provided, however, that if such default is not capable of cure within ten (10) calendar days and Borrower has commenced cure within such period and is diligently pursuing cure, the cure period shall be extended for an additional twenty (20) calendar days (thirty (30) calendar days total).

**(c) Representation Default.** Any representation or warranty made by Borrower in any Loan Document proves to have been false or misleading in any material respect when made.

**(d) Insolvency.** Borrower: (i) becomes insolvent; (ii) files a voluntary petition in bankruptcy; (iii) is adjudicated bankrupt; (iv) makes a general assignment for the benefit of creditors; (v) has an involuntary petition in bankruptcy filed against Borrower that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days; or (vi) has a receiver, trustee, or liquidator appointed for all or substantially all of Borrower's assets.

**(e) Title Defect.** A lien, encumbrance, or other title defect not approved by Lender is discovered or arises against the Property and is not removed, bonded, or insured over within thirty (30) days.

**(f) Judgment.** A judgment or judgments in excess of $[JUDGMENT THRESHOLD] in the aggregate are entered against Borrower and remain unsatisfied for thirty (30) days.

**(i) Cross-Default.** An event of default occurs under any other Loan Document.
